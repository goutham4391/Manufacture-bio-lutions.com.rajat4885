INNOVATION AS A KEY RESOURCE

Research and Development  Center, Schwedt/Oder, Germany

In Schwedt/Oder, a small but forward-thinking community in the northeast of Germany, BIO-LUTIONS has been operating its R&D centre since 2019. Here, we collect, analyze and experiment with raw materials from around the globe in preparation for entering new markets and regions.

Research Center, Schwedt, Germany

In Schwedt, a small but forward-thinking community in the northeast of Germany, BIO-LUTIONS has been operating its technical research centre since 2019. Here, we collect, analyze and experiment with raw materials from around the globe in preparation for entering new markets and regions.

QUALITY PACKAGING
MADE IN GERMANY

Production facility, Schwedt

The production facility in Schwedt/Oder is primarily dedicated to the production of ready to order disposables and packaging solutions. Our local team has extensive experience in fibre-based product manufacturing. Also, the location is ideal thanks to the variety of raw materials available in the region. Furthermore, in 2021 EU-wide legislation went into effect banning the use of many single-use plastic items, increasing the market pressure for sustainable solutions.

ADDRESSING THE PLASTIC WASTE PROBLEM IN INDIA 

Production Facility, Bangalore

In 2018, BIO-LUTIONS opened its first industrial-scale test production facility near Bangalore, India. Here, we focus mainly on the production of biodegradable disposables such as tableware and food packaging. The Bangalore region offers diverse raw materials such as banana stems and sugar cane leaves, which we can source from local small farmers. In addition to ideal raw material availability, India’s single-use plastic ban legislation will go into effect in 2022, further improving the market climate.

SUSTAINABILITY MADE IN CHINA 

Production facility, Jinan

With its rich nature and diverse, abundant agricultural areas and growing population, we find both product demand and reliable raw material availability in Jinan. In addition, social and political awareness of the plastic waste problem is continually growing. Since 2018, China has introduced regulations on the import of post-consumer plastic waste and the use of plastic disposables. Therefore, we consider China an ideal country to produce and market our products.

YOUR PATH TO FIBRE SOLUTIONS

Our goal is to introduce our fibcro® natural fibres all across the globe through decentralised production sites, as well as business partnerships. Are you looking to be a part of our goal to renaturate the world of materials? Then there are a few ways for you to do so. You can, of course, directly place an order for our single-use disposables collection. In addition, if you are interested in a more active role in the BIO-LUTIONS expansion, such as customised products, technology licensing or a joint venture, please reach out to us.
