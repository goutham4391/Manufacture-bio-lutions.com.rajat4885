FIBCRO®
NATURAL FIBRES

BIO-LUTIONS is a Hamburg-based CleanTech company that mechanically converts agricultural residues into unique, self-binding, durable fibcro® natural fibres. This patented method eliminates the need for binding agents or chemical cellulose isolation.

Our current collection of products focuses on biodegradable and compostable single-use disposables and packaging. Our fibcro® natural fibre products are an affordable and scalable alternative to single-use plastic. Contact us now to discover tailor-made solutions for you and your business to reduce plastic waste.

LOCAL PRODUCTION,
LOCAL SOURCING

The concept of decentralised production is a fundamental aspect of the BIO-LUTIONS philosophy because sustainable materials must be sustainably sourced. Producing our biodegradable single-use disposables and packaging in close cooperation with local suppliers, farmers and growers is very important to us. By creating local sourcing and production networks for our fibcro® natural fibres, we can minimise transport distances and keep value creation in the region, thus filling the gap towards a truly circular economy. Decentralised sourcing also ensures diverse and flexible material acquisition, making us more resilient to potential individual local crop shortages.

INCREDIBLE FLEXIBILITY OFFERED BY NATURE

Our process does not depend on a singular crop or plant. Instead, we adapt the formulation to the agricultural residues available in the region. Today, we can already process a wide range of agricultural residues such as wheat straw, hemp shives, nettle, reed, banana stems, vine shoots, and many more. This flexibility in raw materials ensures our ability to source local raw materials independent of country, region or crop season. To additionally ensure sustainable sourcing, we do not use materials that are part of the human food chain. Furthermore, working with agricultural residues makes us independent from the established supply chains of commodities.

CONTINUOUS FIBER INNOVATION

The potential of our fibcro® natural fibres is vast and ever-growing. Our team in Schwedt/Oder, Germany, works closely together with experts from universities and renowned research centres towards developing new and innovative ways to process natural fibres. Moreover, at our BIO-LUTIONS research and development centre in Schwedt/Oder, Germany, we continue to explore material capabilities, improve material performance and thoroughly test new product applications in packaging and beyond.

We support the Sustainable Development Goals

We consider ourselves as one part of a global effort towards restoring a healthy and thriving planet for our children and all generations to come. The global issues of plastic waste pollution and climate change need to influence personal choices and steer business agenda as well. That is why we at BIO-LUTIONS actively work towards the UN Sustainable Development Goals.  

“Ecology and Economy, Visions and Implementation, Global and Local, Size and Impact; those words accompany us all the time in our constant search to make the world more sustainable through natural materials and solutions. It is our responsibility with the next generations!”

Eduardo Gordillo, Founder & CEO
